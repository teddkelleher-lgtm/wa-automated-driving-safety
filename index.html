Method

Simple, transparent arithmetic

This animation compresses the last 30 days into 30 seconds, then advances in real time. It now uses one ball per estimated event in this 30-day window. Death balls are deliberately drawn much larger for emphasis, so size is not area-proportional across categories.

For each category, the right vessel applies: baseline × (1 - automated share × safety effect)

  • No-injury crashes use an 88% safety effect, mapped from Waymo and Swiss Re's property-damage claim comparison.
  • Injury crashes and deaths use a 92% safety effect, mapped from Waymo and Swiss Re's bodily-injury claim comparison.
  • Unknown-severity crashes are excluded from the ball mix: 2,971 in 2024.
  • Pedestrian-marked balls are a highlighted subset from WSDOT's 2024 Pedestrian Involved report. They are included in the main totals, not added on top.
