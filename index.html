Method

Simple, transparent arithmetic

This animation compresses the chosen window into 30 seconds, then advances in real time. The site automatically picks the longest window from 1, 2, 7, 14, 30, and 60 days that still lets the current geography render with one ball per crash at a readable size on the current screen.

  • Traffic deaths and serious injuries come directly from the FHWA performance dashboard, using 2023 data and the latest prior year only if a 2023 serious-injury value is missing.
  • Lower-severity crash counts are estimated from inferred vehicle miles traveled and the 2023 national NHTSA crash rates.
  • Serious-injury crashes and fatal-crash balls are estimated from injury and death counts, so the cards show people while the vessel balls show crashes.
  • The right vessel applies an 88% reduction to no-injury crashes and a 92% reduction to injury and fatal crashes.
