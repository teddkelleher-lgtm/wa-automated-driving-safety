Method

Simple, transparent arithmetic

  • Traffic deaths and serious injuries come directly from the FHWA performance dashboard, using 2023 data and the latest prior year only if a 2023 serious-injury value is missing.
  • Lower-severity crash counts are estimated from inferred vehicle miles traveled and the 2023 national NHTSA crash rates.
  • Serious-injury crashes are estimated from injury counts, while the red balls show individual traffic deaths. Fatal crashes are removed from the vessel so deaths are not double-counted as both a crash ball and a death ball.
  • The right vessel applies an 88% reduction to no-injury crashes and a 92% reduction to injury crashes and deaths.
